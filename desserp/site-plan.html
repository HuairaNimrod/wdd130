Content

Home page

© desserP

Many of the actual webpages that host recipes for desserts are full of ads that creates in the user an unpleasing experience. We will provide a good user experience with no ads, and with the recipes the users will love, for this first phase of the project the recipies will be provided for the host of the admin of the webpage. The Page will have only international recipes

Images for the Home page

Recipes

Each recipe has a list of ingredients, the making process will be divided in steps and will have pictures of how the dessert will look like at the end of the preparation. Additionaly, some insights will be provided in each recipes in the case the ingredient is difficult to find, another possible content could be to include some adresses of stores where a specific ingredient could be find for the user. The required equipment will be also listed and shown in pictures to help the users know what they need.

Images for Recipe Page

Products Reference

This section will contain a list of products used in different recipes that are or could be uncommon for the user, for each item a name of the product and a picture will be displayed. A link for a webpage where the item coud be find will be aso display as well as a common stores where they are usually sell.
